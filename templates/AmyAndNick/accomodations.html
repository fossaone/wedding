Our wish is for everyone to stay onsite with us!

Help us maximize fun and minimize boozy driving on dark windy roads.

  • Located high among the redwood trees
  • Meals are included and will be served at the main lodge
  • There is minimal cell service, but WiFi available
  • Each heated cabin sleeps 14 people, bunk bed style
  • Each cabin has private showers and bathrooms
  • Each bunk has designated outlets, shelving, and lamp

Cost for staying at the cabins at Camp Campbell

  • Entire weekend - $195 per person (includes all food and drinks)
  • 1 night only - $95 per person (includes lunch and drinks)
  • Staying somewhere else? You can purchase breakfast and lunch for Sat. at camp for $30 per person

You can pay for the cabins or food via the PayPal link below or by sending in a check with your RSVP card. Please do so by March 20th.
